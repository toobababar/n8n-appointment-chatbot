# XYZ Medical Clinic – Appointment FAQ  

## Scheduling Appointments  

### How do I book an appointment?  
* You can book online via the **Patient Portal** or call the clinic reception.  

### Do you accept walk-ins?  
* Limited walk-in slots are available daily, but scheduled appointments are preferred.  

### How far in advance should I book?  
* For routine check-ups, book **2–3 weeks in advance**.  
* Same-day appointments may be available for urgent needs.  

---  

## Rescheduling & Cancellations  

### How do I reschedule an appointment?  
* Log in to the **Patient Portal** or call the clinic reception to reschedule.  

### What is the cancellation policy?  
* Please cancel at least **24 hours in advance** to avoid a no-show fee.  

### Is there a fee for missed appointments?  
* Yes, a **\$25 fee** applies for no-shows or late cancellations.  

---  

## Appointment Logistics  

### What should I bring to my appointment?  
* A valid **photo ID**  
* **Insurance card**  
* Any **current medications** or list of prescriptions  

### How early should I arrive?  
* Please arrive **15 minutes before** your appointment time.  

### Do you offer telehealth visits?  
* Yes, telehealth is available for select services.  

---  

## Insurance & Billing  

### Do you accept my insurance?  
* The clinic accepts most major insurance plans. Please check with reception for confirmation.  

### Can I pay out-of-pocket?  
* Yes, self-pay is accepted. Payment is due at the time of service.  

### How do I update my insurance information?  
* Upload details via the **Patient Portal** or provide updated cards at check-in.  

---  

## Special Requests  

### Can I request a specific doctor?  
* Yes, you may request a preferred provider when booking, subject to availability.  

### Do you offer interpreter services?  
* Yes, interpreters can be arranged if requested in advance.  

### Can someone accompany me to the appointment?  
* Yes, one support person is allowed unless otherwise restricted.
